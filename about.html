Foundation

A decade of senior leadership that made all of it possible.

The range and precision of Dominique's work today was shaped by a decade in senior leadership at a national HR services organization — an environment that required advising across more than 1,100 client companies and 26,000 employees simultaneously, spanning industries, company sizes, and HR challenges of every kind.

Those clients spanned every industry: healthcare, manufacturing, professional services, nonprofit, hospitality, retail, and more. They ranged from early-stage companies building their first HR function to established mid-market organizations managing multi-state workforces. No two situations were the same, and the volume meant there was no room to be anything but sharp.

That environment required genuine fluency across the full spectrum of HR: employment law and multi-state compliance, employee relations and investigations, leave administration, performance management, leadership development, HRIS implementation, and org design. The expertise she brings to clients today was not assembled over time. It was built all at once, under pressure, at scale.

What that decade produced was a practitioner who has seen nearly every people challenge an organization can face and knows how to respond to each of them with precision. That is the foundation she brings to every engagement today.
