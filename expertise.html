What I work on

My work covers the full range of people and HR challenges — from building the right systems as you grow, to handling the situations that keep leaders up at night.

People & Organizational Design

I help organizations build the structures and systems that let people do their best work — and that hold up as the company grows or changes. This means thinking through how teams are organized, how accountability works, and how HR operations actually run day to day.

  • Performance management systems
  • Organizational design and role clarity
  • People infrastructure for startups and high-growth companies
  • Workforce planning and analytics
  • HR operations and technology
  • Building and developing internal HR teams

Risk, Compliance & Employee Relations

HR issues don't stay small for long if they're not handled well. I help organizations stay ahead of people-related risk — through solid policies, thoughtful escalation frameworks, and consistent decision-making that holds up under scrutiny.

  • Multi-state and federal employment compliance
  • Employee relations and issue resolution
  • Workplace investigations
  • Leave of absence administration
  • Policy design and governance
